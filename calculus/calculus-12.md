## Problem 3

### Subproblem A

$$f(\alpha_1x_1+\dots+\alpha_n x_n)\leq \alpha f(x_1)+\dots+\alpha_n f(x_n)$$

### Subproblem B

$$f(\alpha x_1 + \dots + \alpha_n x_n)\leq \alpha_1 f(x_1)+\dots+\alpha_n f(x_n)$$
